Consulting Service


Consulting for Public and Private Sectors:

  • Governmental Entities & State Authorities: Strategic and operational guidance.
  • Private Sector Companies: Tailored consulting to meet specific business needs.

Sustainability Expertise:

  • Building Sustainable Value Chains: Creating and managing ecological value chains.
  • Environmental Sustainability Consulting: Advising on sustainable environmental practices.
  • Agricultural Management Consulting: Providing expertise in sustainable agricultural management.

Strategic & Operational Guidance:

  • Strategic Consulting: Long-term planning and strategy development.
  • Operational Consulting: Enhancing day-to-day operations for efficiency and effectiveness.

Food Security & Sustainable Development:

  • Policy Advice: Advising on policies related to food security and sustainable value chains.

Collaboration Facilitation:

  • Public-Private Dialogue (PPD) Establishment: Facilitating dialogue between public and private sectors.
  • Public-Private Partnership (PPP) Development: Developing and managing PPP initiatives.

Non-Profit Consulting Services:

  • Strategic Plan Development: Collaborating with NGOs to create robust strategic plans aligned with their mission and objectives.
  • Capacity Assessments and Building: Conducting comprehensive assessments and targeted initiatives to strengthen organizational capabilities.
  • Program and Intervention Design: Assisting in designing evidence-based programs addressing pressing social issues in Bangladesh.
  • Financial Guidance: Providing guidance on budgeting, financial reporting, and compliance to ensure transparency and accountability.
  • Funding and Partnerships: Supporting NGOs in identifying funding opportunities, preparing grant proposals, and establishing partnerships with donors and stakeholders.
  • Policy Analysis and Advocacy: Assisting in policy analysis, stakeholder mapping, and developing advocacy strategies.
