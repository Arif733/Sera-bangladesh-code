About Us

About SERA Bangladesh

Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Dhaka, SERA Bangladesh is a leading organization for sustainable development, empowers a diverse clientele—government entities, non-profits, private businesses, and individuals—through its multifaceted consulting services. Our unwavering commitment extends across the economic and social landscape, fostering collaboration for positive change.

We distinguish ourselves through our cutting-edge approach, leveraging the transformative power of the Internet of Things (IoT) and traceability solutions to drive efficiency and transparency. Our comprehensive suite of services encompasses project management across various sectors, digital transformation consulting, and custom software development. Furthermore, SERA Bangladesh offers expertise in sustainability consulting, strategic planning, and food security. We support non-profits with capacity building and program design, and bridge the gap to funding. Notably, we assist with organic certification, agricultural research, and market entry for both domestic and foreign businesses. SERA Bangladesh further fuels progress through educational programs on critical sustainability topics.

Meet the Team

SERA Bangladesh is an organization deeply committed to the belief that its people are the cornerstone of its success, a principle that has consistently been validated. The team members are exceptionally talented individually, yet it is the synergy of these talents, aligned with the company's vision, that distinguishes the workplace as exceptional.

The SERA Bangladesh team includes a skilled and collaborative group of highly capable members, all united by the shared goal of delivering top-quality services. The organization prioritizes creating a workplace that is both enjoyable and welcoming while also fostering personal and career advancement.

The success of SERA Bangladesh is rooted in adherence to the core values that drive the organization. These values support collaboration and foster an inspiring and stimulating work environment. SERA Bangladesh takes immense pride in the dedication and exemplary performance of its team and is confident in its hardworking and creatively intelligent team to lead SERA Bangladesh to an even brighter future.
