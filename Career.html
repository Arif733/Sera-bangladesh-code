Programme Officer

Position Overview:

SERA Bangladesh is seeking a dynamic and experienced Programme Officer to join the team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for planning, implementing, and monitoring projects aligned with our mission. This role requires strong analytical skills, excellent communication abilities, and a passion for sustainable development.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Economics, Development Studies, Social Sciences, or a related field. A Master’s degree is preferred.
  • Minimum of two years of relevant experience in project management or a similar role.
  • Strong understanding of project management tools and techniques.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in both English and Bangla.
  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite and data analysis software.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Support program management, execution, and coordination across teams and stakeholders.
  • Contribute to project planning, set objectives, and track progress against milestones and deliverables.
  • Maintain comprehensive program documentation, prepare reports, and ensure effective communication with stakeholders.
  • Assist in budget tracking, expense control, and resource allocation.
  • Foster collaboration among project teams, stakeholders, and partners, and coordinate program-related events.
  • Provide administrative support including meeting coordination, communication, and contract management.
  • Other duties assigned by the Management.

Compensation & Other Benefits:

  • Working Days and Hours: Sunday to Thursday (excluding public holidays)
  • Lunch Facilities: Full Subsidize
  • Salary Review: Yearly
  • Festival Bonus: 2

Finance and Admin

Position Overview:

SERA Bangladesh is seeking a dynamic and experienced Finance and Admin Officer to join the team. Officer - Finance and Admin will be responsible for providing administrative and financial management support in terms of allocating financial and logistical resources to the team for the effective implementation of planned activities at field level under various project. He/she will closely work with the project team to ensure project activities are done through cost efficient programme delivery ensuring donor compliance. S/he will also coordinate with partner teams if necessary to ensure effective implementation of joint events.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field.
  • Minimum of two years of relevant experience in finance and administration, preferably in a corporate or project-based environment.
  • Strong understanding of accounting principles and financial analysis.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks, SAP).
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills.
  • Attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Prepare, analyze, and interpret financial statements, including management accounts, budgets, and variance analysis.
  • Oversee daily financial transactions, bank reconciliations, ledger reconciliations, and ensure accurate accounting entries.
  • Manage accounts receivable, accounts payable, petty cash, and working capital.
  • Establish and maintain strong internal controls to safeguard assets and ensure compliance.
  • Contribute to strategic financial planning, including tax planning and management.
  • Coordinate administrative tasks, such as filing, document management, and office supplies.
  • Provide general administrative support to the management team.
  • Assist with HR functions, including recruitment, onboarding, and employee records.
  • Manage office operations and facilities.
  • Collaborate with project teams to provide financial support and analysis.
  • Track project budgets and expenditures.
  • Assist with financial reporting for projects.
  • Other duties assigned by the Management.

Compensation & Other Benefits:

  • Working Days and Hours: Sunday to Thursday (excluding public holidays)
  • Lunch Facilities: Full Subsidize
  • Salary Review: Yearly
  • Festival Bonus: 2
