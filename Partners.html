Our Partners

  • SourceTrace

    SourceTrace, an innovative Agtech company offers DATAGREEN, a digital platform built on cutting-edge technologies such as Mobile, Blockchain, Remote Sensing & AI/ML offered for food majors, food importers/exporters, logistics providers, governments, NGOs and farmer groups.

  • a2i

    a2i is a multinational digital transformation catalyst from the Government of Bangladesh. It was conceived at the Prime Minister’s Office in 2008 as the flagship program of the government’s widely acclaimed Digital Bangladesh 2021 initiative, with technical support from UNDP.

  • iDEA TREE

    iDEA TREE is a multidisciplinary consulting and project management firm dedicated to development and growth. They bridge the needs of executives and processes, serving as long-term advisors to business leaders. iDEA TREE offers baseline studies for pilot projects, tailored training programs, and audit services.

  • The University of Northampton

    The University of Northampton is a public university based in Northampton, Northamptonshire, England. It was formed in 1999 by the amalgamation of a number of training colleges, and gained full university status as the University of Northampton in 2005.

  • Intertrop

    Intertrop, based in Stuttgart since 2015, is an agricultural consulting firm specializing in global projects in developing countries. We provide expertise in agriculture, environment, sustainability, rural development, value chain development, and consultancy for training programs and groups to Germany. As a University of Hohenheim spin-off, we have a strong agricultural science foundation and close university ties. Our expertise, network, and flexibility distinguish us.

  • Scientific Strategy Consultant Ltd.

    The laboratory industry faces unprecedented levels of change and complexity. With a full team of laboratory consultants, SSCL-BD help labs solve complex issues, pursue opportunities, achieve measurable cost savings, and serve their patients and stakeholders better through our laboratory consulting services and other service areas.

  • CargoStar Bangladesh

    CargoStar Bangladesh, recognized as a top cargo agent by Emirates Sky Cargo, is a premier international freight forwarding company. Led by experienced local experts, the company focuses on building lasting service relationships and ensuring seamless shipment logistics. Partnering with reliable international counterparts, CargoStar Bangladesh guarantees professional and careful handling of all shipments to meet customer needs.
